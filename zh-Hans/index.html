Privacy Policy

Effective 2026-06-27

Inquest does not collect any personal data. The app has no analytics, no tracking, no third-party SDKs, and no backend server. It works fully offline after installation.

Data the app stores on your device only

The app saves the following entirely on your device, never sent anywhere:

  • Your detective rating and streak.
  • Which cases you've attempted (so you can't redo a case for full rank credit).
  • Your in-progress notes for the current case.
  • Your settings (theme, locale override, hint preferences).

Game Center (optional)

If you sign in to Game Center, Apple handles the connection — Inquest only writes your solve-time, rating, and streak to your Game Center profile so leaderboards can render. We don't see your Game Center ID or any other Apple account data. Game Center is optional; the app works without it.

Third parties

No third-party SDKs collect data in this app. The only system-managed SDK present is Google's in-app review prompt on Android (the same Apple "rate this app" sheet on iOS), which lets the operating system show a rating dialog at a player's delight peak. Neither prompt sends gameplay or device data anywhere on the app's behalf.

Children

The app's rating is 12+ due to fictional murder-mystery text content. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes

If we ever change this policy, the new version replaces this page and the date at the top updates.
