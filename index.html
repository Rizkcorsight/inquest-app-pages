INQUEST
a daily detective game

One murder. One day. Solve it.

Every day, a fresh case opens on your phone at local midnight. Same victim, same suspects, same evidence — every detective worldwide working the same file by their own local date. You have 24 hours before the next one drops.

What you do

  • Open the file. Examine evidence at the scene. Read the coroner's report. Every case is fair-play: the solution is always derivable from what you can see.
  • Interview the suspects. Pick three questions per suspect from a curated menu.
  • Pin your theory. Take notes. Connect clues. Pin the contradiction that breaks an alibi.
  • File your report. Name the culprit, the weapon, the place, the motive. Get it right on the first try for the cleanest result.
  • Climb the ranks. Constable → Sergeant → Inspector → Chief Inspector → Sherlock.
  • Share without spoilers. Compare your solve with friends — your story, not the killer.

Why Inquest is different

  • A full year of cases at launch — 365 mysteries, every one validated by a built-in fair-play solver.
  • Six languages day-one: English, Spanish, French, German, Japanese, Simplified Chinese — translated for narrative, not just menus.
  • Five twist types: false alibis, mistaken identity, accomplice cover, wrong weapon, suicide staged as murder.
  • Settings worldwide. Tokyo film studios. Lagos art galleries. Reykjavík dive resorts. Renaissance Venice. Victorian London. Modern São Paulo.
  • Game Center leaderboards: daily solve time, all-time rank, longest streak.
  • No subscription. No ads. A 3-day full-access trial, then a one-time unlock at the store-displayed local price.
